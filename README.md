
## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data with the aim to explore revenue patterns, product performance, and customer trends.

## 🧰 Tools Used
- SQL – For querying and preparing the data
- Snowflake - A platform to execute SQL code
- Excel – For data cleaning and pivot table analysis  
  

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Revenue grew steadily from January(12%) to June(24%) showing rising demand in differennt seasons. 
- Monday and Friday are peak revenue days, while other days are steady, and Saturday dips slightly.
- Morning sales are recorded at 55,6% dominating revenue. However, with a sharp drop after 10 AM.
- The top 10 best selling products generate 25% of the total revenue, driven mostly by Coffee and Tea.

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on running breakfast combo specials to maximize high - demand periods.  
- Promote  a 1 hour limited - time offer and a loyalty reward points system.
- Staff roster must be in shifts so we can pay staff salaries accordingly since we know peak and off peak hours.
- Prioritize top performing products and review low sellers, this will help us save some funds.
